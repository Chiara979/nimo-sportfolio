Epidermitecture

We all have seen biopatina, yet have you ever noticed it?

In this work, we took on a different perspective by switching to a non human point of view. Looking from microscopic to systematic, we talked about coexistence amongst seemingly unrelated entities and how they come together to reimagine the surrounding environment. Many people may view them as insignificant stains on houses and other surfaces. However, the biopatina is incredibly valuable and exceptional. Biopatina is the formation of a unique complex symbiotic relationship of multiple organisms on human made surfaces. The biopatina is not only beneficial towards each other, but also towards the surface it lives on, helping in preserving it.
