Moss Mirror

I designed a mirror that has an emotional value for the consumer. It is based on the concept of epidermitecture and the co living of humans and biopatina. On top of the mirror is a see through print picture of biopatin that reminds the viewer of it’s existence and it’s teachings. On the frame the moss has to be kept alive by the consumer through spraying it with water and in return the moss improves the air conditions of the room.
